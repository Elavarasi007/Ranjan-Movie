The Story

JUSTICE HAS A
NEW VOICE

Ranjan is a gripping courtroom drama and legal thriller that follows the journey of a highly respected advocate known for his sharp legal mind and unwavering commitment to justice. When a controversial case exposes corruption, abuse of power, and the influence of powerful individuals, Ranjan steps into the courtroom to fight for the truth. As evidence unfolds and tensions rise, the battle becomes more than a legal case—it becomes a fight for justice itself.
